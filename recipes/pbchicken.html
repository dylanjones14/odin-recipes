Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Ketjap
  • Peanut butter
  • Stock cube
  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Ground cumin
  • Ground coriander
  • Sambal Badjak

Steps

  1. Fry the chicken until brown then remove
  2. Fry the onion
  3. Then the garlic
  4. Then add the spices
  5. Then the PB + ketjap
  6. Then add the chicken back in
  7. Then add the stock
  8. Add sambal & season to taste
  9. Simmer for 15 mins
